About us


Our job is to inform people on how to prevent house fires, saving the homes, and lives of people. The safety of you and your home is very important for your survival. With the knowledge from our website you can keep these two things safe, and also reduce the amount of work our firefighters, who already work long hours and risk their lives everyday just making sure our lives are safe.

If a fire has broken out in your home, make sure to call 911 or any local emergency services.
